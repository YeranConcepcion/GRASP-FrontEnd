How to Use GRASP

Welcome to GRASP, your go-to platform for finding nearby gas stations with up-to-date fuel prices in Puerto Rico. Whether you're trying to find the cheapest fuel or locate a specific gas station, GRASP makes it easy for you.

1. Filter the Results:

On the left side of the screen, you’ll find three filters that help you refine your search:

  • By Price: Select this option to sort the stations by fuel prices, from lowest to highest.
  • By Brand: Choose a fuel station brand if you prefer a specific one.
  • By Gas Type: Filter by the type of fuel you need, such as Regular, Diesel, or Premium.

2. View Nearest Stations:

Below the filters, you’ll see a list of the Nearest Stations with their prices. These are the closest stations to your current location based on the filter you have selected.

  • Gulf & Garage Wilmar 91.2
  • Mobil - Gas Station 92.7
  • TotalEnergies Barrio Cacao Bajo 93.7
  • Ecomaxx Patillas El Cerrito 93.9

3. Click on a Station for More Information:

When you click on a station name from the list, a popup will appear showing more details about the station.

  • You’ll see the station’s name, rating, and available fuel types with their prices.
  • In the popup, you can also click the Edit Price button if you have permission to update the station prices.

4. Get Directions to the Station:

To get directions to the station, simply click the Located in [City Name] button at the bottom of the popup. This will redirect you to Google Maps, showing you the station's location and guiding you there.
